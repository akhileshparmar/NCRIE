Dear Colleagues!

It is our pleasure to invite you to the 2020 National Conference on Research and Innovation in Education (NCRIE 2020) that will take place at CSE/IT, SIRT, SAGE University, Indore (Online Mode) , Madhya Pradesh, India. This conference provides opportunities for the researchers, academicians, students and delegates to exchange new ideas and application experiences face to face, to establish research relations and to find global partners for future collaborations. We hope that the conference results will contribute significantly to the knowledge in the field of interdisciplinary research like innovations and research in different field of education and its multiple streams like Engineering (CSE, IT, EC, ME, CIVIL), Management, Agriculture, Sciences, Basic Applied Science, Commerce, Pharmacy etc.

Visit site: www.sageuniversity.in
